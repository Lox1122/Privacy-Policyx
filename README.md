Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Extreme GFX respects user privacy. This application does not collect, store, sell, rent, or send user personal information to our servers.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Extreme GFX does not collect:
- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Identity information
- Contacts
- Messages
- Photos
- Videos
- Files
- Voice recordings
- Precise location data
- Passwords
- Banking or payment information

Extreme GFX does not transmit your personal data to us.

2. Local Device Data Used for App Features

To provide app functionality, Extreme GFX may use certain information locally on your device only, including:
- App settings and preferences
- Sound limiter preferences
- Helper bubble settings
- Custom labels created inside the app
- Device information
- System status information
- Foreground app detection, if the user grants Usage Access permission

This information stays on the device and is not sent to our servers.

3. Advertising

Extreme GFX itself does not collect user personal data. However, the app may display ads through third-party advertising services. When ads are shown, advertising providers may process certain technical data through their own systems.

This may include:
- Advertising ID
- Device information
- IP address
- Network information
- Ad interaction data

This processing is handled only by the advertising provider under its own privacy policy. Extreme GFX does not collect or store this data on its own servers.

Unity Ads Privacy Policy:
https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

4. Permissions

Some app features may require Android permissions. These permissions are used only to make app features work on the device.

These permissions are not used:
- to collect personal data for us
- to send user data to our servers
- to build personal profiles

5. Data Sharing

Extreme GFX does not sell, rent, trade, or share user personal information with third parties for its own purposes.

6. Data Storage

App settings may be stored locally on the device. We do not maintain a remote database containing user personal information.

7. User Control

Users may at any time:
- disable app permissions
- clear app data
- uninstall the app

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If updated, the date at the top will be changed.
